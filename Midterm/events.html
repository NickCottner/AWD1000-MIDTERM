What Else Do We Do?


Chili Cookoff

Chili? What the heck does chili have to do with running. Nothing. But the Belleville Chili Cookoff is a fun event, it’s currently our major fundraiser, and it’s a great way to get our name out to new people.

River to River Relay

River to River Relay is held in April of each year and the BRC has entered teams for a decade..

Adopt a Trail

Don’t forget to pick up any litter you see along the trail and keep an eye out for anything that needs to be taken care of (potholes, lights out, etc). Send a email to us and we’ll forward the info to Parks and Recreation.
