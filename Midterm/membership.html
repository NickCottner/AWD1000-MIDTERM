Want To Be A Member?

BRC membership registration provides many benefits and is open for new memberships and renewal of memberships for 2021 at BRC Signup or by submitting an application (BRC Membership Application). A paid, registered membership is not required to join BRC group runs and most club activities, but it does provide many benefits, including discounts at area races and BRC gear (see more complete list of benefits below).
Not sure if your membership is currently valid? Please email Sarah West (BRC Membership Committee Chair).
